inTEUtion

inTEUtion is purpose-built for NVOCCs to manage the complete lifecycle of every container - engineered not just for the digital age, but for the modern information age. It includes dedicated modules for Equipment Management, Capacity Planning, EXIM operations, and CMNR (Container Maintenance & Repair).

Product Core Capabilities

Industry-Leading Operations

The platform includes powerful capabilities such as a powerful tariff/pricing engine, automated demurrage/detention management, real-time visibility dashboards, robust EDI integrations, and granular job-level profitability tracking.

Tariff & Pricing Engine
Powerful logic resolving dynamic regional tariffs and contract rates automatically based on event updates.
Event(Move)-Based Tariffs
Automatically provisions shipping rates, local port surcharges, and temporal container demurrages/detentions the moment a move is updated, ensuring zero unbilled leakage.
Visibility Dashboards
Instant dynamic monitoring tracking global fleet container flows, logistics timelines, and system metrics.
Robust EDI Integrations
Sleek electronic data interchange exchanging data automatically with carriers, depot partners, and customs gateways.
Job-Level Profitability
Granular real-time accounting and provisional margin tracking mapping actual vs estimated costs per unit booking.
Agency Settlement
Tracks regional collections, agent commissions, and Disbursement Accounts (DA) within a custom time window for simple 1-click leakage-free closure.
Customizable & Extensible
Adaptable architecture built to accomodate custom operational requirements and tailored NVOCC business models.
Third-Party Integration Ready
Our modern API based architecture enables custom integration with external partners and systems ensuring seamless data exchange
Reports
Module-level reports deliver actionable insights on container movement, business flow, container availability, cost accrual, and more - enabling data-driven decisions and business continuity.
User Management & Authentication
inTEUtion features granular access management with configurable user roles and permissions, ensuring robust security and controlled access tailored to each user's function.
